About

I'm Sinan, Turkish and American. I grew up in Istanbul and moved to New York in March 2026.

My first sales job was at 14, working at a friend's family stand inside the Grand Bazaar selling jewelry to tourists. Three different languages, a small piece of foot traffic, and the hourly reminder that the customer in front of you can walk away in two seconds. That's where the muscle started.

Six years of outbound have followed since. At 20, I cold-called 6,000 villa owners during COVID and signed 85 paying partners on a third-party hygiene certification I built from scratch. Later I ran a small Amazon growth agency, acquiring clients cold and retaining them with 128% net revenue retention. Most recently I'm a BDR at Stuut, an a16z-backed AI accounts receivable platform, running 200+ dials a day into finance leaders.

Alongside that, I run growth across three D2C consumer brands: Marcus Pierce (men's apparel), Reverie (supplements), and Vicarious (artisan peanut butter). My day-to-day is paid media, customer discovery, retention, and the unglamorous operational work that makes brands compound.

The thread through all of it is the same. I like work where the feedback loop is short and the result is honest. Sales does that better than anything else I've found.
